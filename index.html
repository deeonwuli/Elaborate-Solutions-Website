Our Services

Established in 2004 (RC: 384949), Elaborate Solutions Limited is an international freight forwarding and shipping service in Nigeria. Since our inception, we have become a story of excellence and reliability.

We have our corporate head office in Lagos and contact offices and partners worldwide. We provide efficient shipping freight forwarding and logistic services in Nigeria and beyond as we are a licensed Customs Clearing Agency.

Our services include, but are not limited to,

Icon

Courier Services

Door-to-door delivery services all over the country.
We are in a vantage position to provide first class door to door cargo services to its numerous customers which lessens and eases logistic problems to the consignee. We also go as far as providing services like cross-country packaging, transporting and shipping and clearing/delivery in the destination country.

Icon

Freight Forwarding

Global freight shipping via air, ocean, road or rail.
Elaborate Solutions Limited has the ability to move goods efficiently with our fully devloped sea, air and land transportation capabilities that improve supply chain flexibility, predictabilty and efficiency. Solutions are tailored to customer needs from single shipments.

Icon

Cargo Haulage

Transportation of heavy and large goods via air and road.
At Elaborate Solutions Limited, we are aware of the need for prompt delivery of goods at client's specifications. Our range of cargo vehicles, manned by trained personnel, regularly transport goods and bulk materials throughout Nigeria.
